# Narrative-Aware ETF Rotation Platform

A quantitative research platform for ETF rotation using momentum, narrative, liquidity, and risk signals.

## Research Question

Can narrative information and risk controls improve the risk-adjusted performance of a traditional ETF momentum rotation strategy while reducing drawdown and unnecessary turnover?

## Planned Modules

1. ETF Universe and Data Pipeline
2. Momentum Baseline
3. Risk Engine
4. Narrative Signal
5. Composite Ranking
6. Portfolio Construction
7. Walk-Forward Backtesting
8. Evaluation and Dashboard
